Ingredients

  • 450g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes (796 ml)
  • 1 can tomato sauce (425 ml)
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 12 lasagne noodles
  • 225g shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 60g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 425g ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagne noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Add the onion and garlic and cook until softened.
  4. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. In a medium bowl, mix the egg, ricotta cheese, and Parmesan cheese together.
  6. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom. Place a layer of lasagne noodles on top of the sauce. Spread a layer of the cheese mixture on top of the noodles, followed by a layer of the meat sauce. Repeat these layers until all ingredients are used up.
  7. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese on top of the last layer of meat sauce.
  8. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Let the lasagne cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
